    How to read this radar
    
  
    
    
    What you are looking at. Eight digital risk domains, plotted by severity across three zones: critical, elevated and managed. The closer to the centre, the more critical. Each signal is colour-coded by its level, and its position moves when you switch sector.
    
    
    Switch sector and role. Use the controls to move between Building Societies, Banks and Insurers, and to tailor the board questions to your role, from CEO to Chair of Audit.
    
    
    Proportionality. Drag the indicative scale slider to a firm size and you will see what proportionate tends to look like at that scale, each traceable to the regulator and framed as a question to test against your own context, alongside worked examples across a weaker to stronger scale. It is not a compliance verdict, and nothing you select leaves your browser.
    
    
    What this is. A thought leadership tool to prompt better board conversations, not a replacement for your risk register. Free, with no login and no personal data collected.
